About Us

Indian Rubber Materials Research Institute (IRMRI) formerly known as Indian Rubber Manufacturers Research Association (IRMRA), Thane, Maharashtra which was established in 1958 as a small scale R&D Institute, has now become an internationally well known Centre of Excellence both for Non-tyre & Tyre sectors.

It is an autonomous body operating under the administrative control of Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Govt. of India.

IRMRI is well known for its expertise in the fields of Testing & Investigations, Research & Product/Material Development, Training and Manpower Development and Consultancy Services, and has diversified its activities in the new emerging areas of Research.

We have 2 other branches in India, The South Centre was established at Sri City to cater to the Rubber and Allied Industry in southern India. The establishment of East Centre was intended to fulfill the needs of the rubber and allied industries.

Quality Policy

To provide Timely, Innovative & World Class services by systematically adhering to Quality Management Systems and Procedures

To undertake research & development and generate IP assets

To design, develop and validate the rubber & allied products as per the customer's requirement

To provide reliable, impartial and traceable testing and research based evaluation

To develop human resource for rubber and allied industry by imparting training and academic program

To train and enhance the competency of the personnel and familiarize with the requirement of documented management system

Committed to comply and continuously improve the effectiveness of the management system, safety, morale and work environment within the organization

Mission & Vision

Vision

To be a committed world class rubber and allied material research, development, testing and training centre with innovative & value added service to customers

Mission

To serve the rubber & allied industries to fulfill their scientific & technological requirements in order to improve their global competitiveness.
